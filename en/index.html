How it works

From badge to insight Upsells

Implementation takes one day. Results from day one.

01

Putting on a badge

The employee takes the badge from the charging station. Recording starts automatically. Two microphones capture both the employee and the customer.

02

Data is transferred

After the change, the badge is charged. All entries automatically uploaded to the server via Wi-Fi.

03

AI analyzes

The neural network transcribes conversations, separates voices, scores 12+ metrics: scripts, conflicts, tone, upsells.

04

You receive a report

Visual dashboard with ratings, problem areas and specific recommendations. Every day, without manual work.
